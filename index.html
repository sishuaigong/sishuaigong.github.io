About Me

I am a final year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, Purdue Univeristy. I am advised by Pedro Fonseca and work with other pepole in the Reliable and Secure Systems Lab. Before joining Purdue, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China in 2019.

I am broadly interested in operating system research, with a particular focus on system reliability and security.
